Hello! I'm Lauren, aka Thy.

Through my years of experience, I've had the opportunity to collaborate with diverse teams, working on designs that balance functionality with aesthetics. I use tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud and Figma to create designs and prototypes.

I thrive on exploring new ideas and solving problems, always committed to delivering impactful solutions that aim to exceed expectations.
